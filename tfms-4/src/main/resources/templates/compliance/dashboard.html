Compliance Module Overview

The Compliance & Regulatory Reporting module ensures that all trade finance transactions (Letter of Credits and Bank Guarantees) meet regulatory, bank internal, and international standards.

What is Compliance?

Compliance means following all applicable rules and regulations. In banking, every transaction must be verified against:

  • Bank Internal Policies: Approved transaction limits, party eligibility
  • Government Regulations: Anti-Money Laundering (AML), Know Your Customer (KYC)
  • International Trade Rules: Export/import restrictions, trade finance standards
  • Sanctions & Restrictions: OFAC, UN, and other international sanctions lists
Why Compliance is Critical
Non-compliance can result in:
  • Heavy regulatory fines and penalties
  • License suspension or revocation
  • Legal action and criminal charges
  • Reputational damage
  • Money laundering and fraud exposure
